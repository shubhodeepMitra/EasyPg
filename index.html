About Us

With the aim of countering residential problems amongst students of The National Institute of Engineering , this DBMS project, namely, EasyPG offers a platform with options to search, compare, contact and verify PG/Flats if eligible for student's occupancy. This database also provides opportunities to PG/Flat owners to get occupants (students) with ease.
This website model, EasyPG would be storing their all possible data of Students and PG owners and PG’s available for rent. That includes information like Student details, PG details and its feature.

  • Removing the middlemen and offering a transparent communication between the interested sides.
  • Encouraging resident-owners to offer competitive prices and features to student occupants.
  • College may use the database to keep residential information of each student.
